# SpiritVale Item Probability Simulator

The **SpiritVale Item Probability Simulator** is a tool I created for the community. It calculates the odds of obtaining specific gear variants through crafting or farming and provides a clear view of how much time and resources are typically required to reach your goals.

## 🛠️ Features

* **Crafting Simulator:** Simulate crafting attempts to see the probability of hitting specific substats and rolls.
* **Farming Simulator:** Calculate your farming efforts by inputting your kill rate and mob density to receive a realistic projection of your time-to-drop.
* **Hybrid View:** This tool connects your farming and crafting data, showing you the estimated time to your item by calculating the combined probability of both methods.
* **Clearer Planning:** Uses confidence intervals (Median, Likely, Safe) to provide a clearer view of the time and effort required to obtain your item.

## :bar_chart: How It Works

1. **Select Your Item:** Choose your equipment type from the dropdown to load the available substats and ranges.
2. **Define Your Goals:** Pick the stats and values you are aiming for. The simulator will immediately show your 1-in-X odds.
3. **Add to List:** Click "Add to Wanted Variants" to move your selection to the **Wanted Variants & Combined Odds** section. The simulator and confidence tables will now use this data to calculate your results.

## :gear: Understanding the Simulator & Analysis

* **Crafting:** Set your material costs and materials/minute to track your potential resource usage and progress.
* **Farming:** Adjust **"Target Mobs"** (the specific mobs that drop the item you're looking for) vs. **"Total Mobs"** (the total mob population in the map) along with your **kills/minute** to match your specific playstyle.
* **Hybrid View:** Use this to see how much faster you can obtain your item when you combine both crafting and farming efforts. It calculates the expected time and resource investment for your goal by accounting for both methods working together.
